Welcome to Frequently Asked Questions!

Q: Do I need to enter a continent and landscape on home page?

A: No. You can simply hit generate, and a random location from around the world will appear!

Q: Can I generate locations with only one field, or do I need both?

A: Our generator provides you with three options: you can fill in both for the most filtered search; you can fill out either the continent or landscape field for a continent or landscape-specific search; or, you can leave both blank and have a completely random search!

Q: Are there multiple photos for each location?

A: Yes! Simply click the generated photo to cycle through the gallery!

Q: Help! Currency is not converting.

A: Be sure to fill in all fields before hitting generate. If you see NaN, then you did not enter an amount.

Q: Can you interact with the Google Map?

A: Yes! You are able to explore the Google Map by clicking and dragging! This lets you see other fun locations, like restaurants, hotels, and shops, near your generated location!

Q: How many locations are included in this app?

A: Great question! There are 90 unique locations from around the world included in this app! See if you can find them all!
